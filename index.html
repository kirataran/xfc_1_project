What should I wear?

Semi-formal attire you can dance in. Granola or Western-themed touches, or even full on cowboy, are encouraged.

Are we going to be camping?

The bride and groom will be staying in a cabin at the venue. If you're interested in camping with us, just let us know in the RSVP! Otherwise, check out the other lodging options in the RSVP.

How do I book a room at the Costanoa Lodge?

To reserve a room, please call Costanoa Lodge directly at 650-879-1100 and select extension 0. Be sure to mention the group name Emily Chen Retreat.

We have blocked out king lodge rooms for our guests. The rate will be approximately $335 per night, and the hotel offers free parking.
